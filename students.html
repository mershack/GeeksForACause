Tutoring

Geeks can help other students by tutoring

Peer Tutoring & Youth tutoring

Since geeks will have a good knowledge base on technolgy they will be encourage to share that knowledge by tutoring. The tutoring would mostly cover classes such as AP Computer Science and any other tech base classes. Geeks will also tutor young students who are in middle school.

Classes covered:

  • Introduction to Computer Science
  • AP Computer Science
  • IT

Tech training

Geeks will be assign to train to those who are less tech inclined. (e.g. eldery individuals, those that can't afford tech to learn it)
