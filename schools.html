Geek Day!

A day when geeks can be geeks

Tech day!

A tech themed carnival hosted by the school. Fun tech relate activites will be provided such as video game competions, including video games that require physical motion, social or interactive music and dance performance, and other high tech activities such as laser tag. This carnvial will be used to fund things such as laptops and other IT equipment for students attending local non-profit child enrichment programs or community access centers

Tech donation drive

A donation drive for old workable IT equipment and software (computers, iPads, etc.) in order to purchase new devices (iPads, laptops, etc.). Students will be able to donate to recieve community service hours and top donors will be recongized.

Hackathons

For an entire week a school will have bins out so that students can donate to geeks who are less fotunate. Students who donate working electronics will be able to recieve community service hours by helping the geek community. Geeks will help by refurbishing electronics that are slightly damaged.

Shark-Tankx

Students will have the opportunity to present their innovative tech ideas to those who are experienced in the tech field. This will help geeks understand what their future in tech will be like.
