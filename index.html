Welcome to Geeks with a cause

A place where geeks can learn how to make the world a better place.

Geeks-with-a-Cause is a non-profit organization devoted to inspiring tech-savvy high school students to mobilize their passion and knowledge of technology for a social change.

Aims

Education

To help educate high school students who are interested in technology.

Geeks and their communities

To help geeks find ways to help their local communities with their knowledge in technology

Tech

To help geeks master technology, have fun, and network with colleagues.

Our Mission



1. The Geek community


We want to unite geeks to build a community where they help each others and also reach out to help the world.









2. Helping the less tech-fortunate


We want to create a platform where geeks can help the less fortunate in their community, by donating their old equipments, or tutoring the less fortunate in their community







3. Let geeks be geeks


We want geeks to be proud to be geeks.
